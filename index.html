Certified by AEO

AEO, or Authorized Economic Operator, is a logistics accreditation ensuring compliance, security, and efficiency in international trade.

Warehousing facilities

Warehousing is a fundamental component of logistics, providing storage space for products before they are shipped to their final destinations.

Transportation Facilities

Transportation is another key aspect, involving the movement of goods through various modes such as trucks, ships, planes, and trains. Logistic facilities strategically located near transportation hubs help streamline the movement of goods and reduce lead times.

Distribution centers

Distribution centers are essential for consolidating, sorting, and packaging products before they are dispatched to retailers or directly to consumers. These centers are designed to handle large volumes of goods efficiently, ensuring timely delivery and minimizing delays.

Fulfillment Centers

Fulfillment centers are a critical component of e-commerce logistics, where orders are processed, picked, packed, and shipped to customers. With the growth of online shopping, fulfillment centers have become increasingly sophisticated, incorporating robotics and automation to expedite order processing.
